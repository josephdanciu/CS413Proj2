Floaters

Game Instructions:

Take your hot air balloon to the other side of the castle on the stand. Watch out for obsticles! If you hit a bird, tree, or the castle you will have to restart. (You can hit the clouds). Controls: Since its a windy day, every up or down move you make will also move you to the right 1 square.

Q: Move 2 up (and 1 right)

W: Move 2 down (and 1 right)

A: Move 1 up (and 1 right)

S: Move 1 down (and 1 right)

D: Move 1 right

Press ENTER to start the game.
