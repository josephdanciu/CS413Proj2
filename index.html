Floaters

Game Instructions:

Take your hot air balloon to the other side of the castle ON THE LANDING PAD (dont just take it past the castle). Watch out for obsticles! If you hit a bird, tree, or the castle you will have to restart. (You can hit the clouds).

Controls:

Q: Move 2 up (and 1 right)

W: Move 2 down (and 1 right)

A: Move 1 up (and 1 right)

S: Move 1 down (and 1 right)

D: Move 1 right
